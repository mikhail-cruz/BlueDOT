Land Degradation

  • Reusing materials help to reduce the requirement of harvesting resources.
  • Reduce the use of pesticides and fertilizers in agricultural activities.
  • Switch to a reusable cloth bag for groceries and avoid buying packaged items.
  • Do proper disposal of garbage.
  • Buy biodegradable products.
  • Do organic gardening and eat organic food.
  • Create dumping ground away from residential areas.
